Anabolic French Toast

A very tasty modifiable recipe for your needs. Lower in calories, high in protein and good for a weight loss phase

Overall a lower calorie version of the popular dish French Toast, achieved by exchanging some ingredients. The recipe itself takes only about 10 minutes in order to reach you gullet.

Ingredients

  • Servings: 1 person.
  • White Bread: Try to find large and thick bread.
  • Egg whites: Egg whites compared to eggs helps to cut down on calories while keeping the protein content.
  • Cinnamon: Gonna add some extra tasty flavors the toast.
  • Artificial sweetener: Let's us add some sweet flavor without adding extra calories that comes from sugar. Choose a sweetener of your choice.
  • Oil Spray: Helps us put a quick layer of oil on the pan, saves us a lot of calories, oil is very calorie dense.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather the ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Put the egg whites (100g of egg whites per bread), cinnamon (2-3 taps of cinnamon) and sweetener (1 tsp) in a bowl, whisk them together.
  3. Step 3: Heat up your pan to medium-high heat.
  4. Step 4: Soak the bread in the mixture, let it absorb for a little.
  5. Step 5: Place the bread on the pan, fry each side for about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Step 6 (optional): Add fruit of your choice and some low fat greek yoghurt mixed with previously mentioned sweetener.
  7. Step 7: Serve and enjoy.
