Anabolic French Toast

A very tasty modifiable recipe for your needs. Lower in calories, high in protein, good for a weight loss phase or even bulking

The recipe itself takes only about 10 minutes in order to reach you gullet.

Ingredients

  • Servings: 1 person.
  • White Bread: Try to find large and thick bread.
  • Egg whites: Egg whites compared to eggs helps to cut down on calories while keeping the protein content.
  • Cinnamon: Gonna add some extra tasty flavors the toast.
  • Artificial sweetener: Let's us add some sweet flavor without adding extra calories that comes from sugar. Choose a sweetener of your choice.
  • Oil Spray: Helps us put a quick layer of oil on the pan, saves us a lot of calories, oil is very calorie dense.
